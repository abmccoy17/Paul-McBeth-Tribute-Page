Paul McBeth

Paul McBeth - 5X world champion

Biography

Paul McBeth (aka McBeast) is an American professional disc golfer. He broke onto the scene in 2005 and been a force to reckon with ever since. His career earnings according to PDGA is $420,677.84 (as of 12-31-2018). Paul McBeth married Hannah Croke on December 30, 2018. From 2005-2018 McBeth was sponsored by Innova Champion Discs. On November 19, 2018 Paul announced a new sponsorship with Discraft for the 2019 season, signing for an extremely lucrative 4 year, 1 million dollar contract.
